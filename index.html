How to Buy




STEP 1

  • Download Trust wallet
  • Install trust wallet on playstore, follow their guild and finish the setup.

    • STEP 2

  • Send BNB to Trust wallet
  • After installing Trust wallet, you can send BNB to your trust wallet address from an exchange.
  • Copy your BNB wallet and send BNB for an exchange like stride.

    • STEP 3

  • Connect to pancake swap
  • Go to Dapps search for pancakeswap
  • Click on pancake swap
  • Click on connect to pancakeswap to connect your wallet.
    • STEP 4

  • You can now exchange your BNB to STRIDE at even amount.
  • Click on the swap button to complete your transaction. Make sure you set fast processing speed in trust wallet.
